Grandma's Lasagna Recipe

Everyone loves a good lasagna, right? It's a great way to feed a crowd and a perfect dish to bring to a potluck. It freezes well. It reheats well. Leftovers will keep you happy for days. Allow the lasagna to cool at least 15 before serving. Leftovers will keep for about 5 days. May be reheated in conventional oven or microwave. Leave aluminum tent on for storage.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna strips
  • Tomatoe sauce
  • Swiss Cheese
  • Ground beef
  • Mozarella Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook Lasagana strips
  2. Cook the ground beef
  3. Lay down the lasagna strips
  4. Pour the Ground beef
  5. Apply tomatoe sauce
  6. Lay the cheese on top
  7. Put it in the oven for 23 minutes
